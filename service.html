Administrative Service

From 2019 to 2023, I worked as an MBA admission/recruitment manager in the University of Louisville College of Business. During this time, I worked on the following:

  • Built the internationalization strategy with the college leadership team, including building collaborations with overseas universities at the undergraduate and graduate levels, creating and customizing specialized master’s programs, building relationships with foreign universities through scholarly and programmatic exchanges, etc.

  • Cultivated the relationships with, and conducted official visits to 4 Chinese universities together with the Interim Dean, to work on building long-term collaborations and signing the Memorandum of Understanding (MOU).

  • Arranged events overseas for the Interim Dean to give a talk to an audience of 200+ local students and teachers, covering topics such as AI and the future of business, building personal success in the era of AI

  • Worked with the Vice Provost for International Affairs and the International Center to represent the university in education fairs abroad

  • Mentored prospective and current international and domestic students on the academic programs to pursue (on campus, online, certificate, dual degree, etc.), and other personal development opportunities such as paid internship program, scholarships, professional development, etc.

  • Trained and onboarded student interns for our office; served as a mentor for students seeking internships and jobs

  • Worked with the Associate Dean for Programs, and the Executive Director of MBA Programs, on strategies to recruit Chinese students. Planned, organized and conducted several recruitment trips to China with leadership, which included official visits to local universities, study abroad agencies, and education fairs across major cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Nanjing, Suzhou, Hangzhou, Ningbo.

  • Together with team, managed 300+ total annual enrollments in several master’s programs. Our team achieved growth in enrollment by 200%.

  • Student service experience through coordination with the Office of Student Services, Career Center, Office of Admissions, International Student and Scholar Services, etc.

  • Utilizing student management software to track students’ progress, including Slate, Radius by Campus Management, Peoplesoft, Keystone Academic Solutions, etc.

  • Created and purchased various marketing materials for overseas and domestic fairs and visits, including landing pages, banners and table cloths, standing displays and tabletop displays, brochures and flyers, branded swags; worked with the Marketing Team to continuously monitor and improve digital marketing strategies
